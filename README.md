## What is Outline?

Outline is a decentralised prediction market protocol where users take a position on whether an asset's price will **stay within** (BOUND) or **break outside** (BREAK) a defined price band by expiry.

Every dollar deposited earns yield via Morpho vaults for the entire duration of the market. At settlement, winners take the pool — losers keep their yield.

## How it works

**Create** — anyone can open a market by seeding both sides, choosing an asset, a band width, and a duration. The market goes live immediately.

**Trade** — pick BOUND if you think the price holds the range, or BREAK if you think it escapes. One side per wallet. Token prices shift dynamically as capital flows in.

**Settle** — once the market expires, the final price is read from a Chainlink oracle. Winners redeem their tokens for USDC. Losers claim the yield their deposit earned while it was locked.

## Why Outline?

Most prediction markets make you lose everything on the wrong side. Outline changes that — even losing positions earn yield. It turns a binary outcome into a better deal for everyone in the pool.
